About Us

We Believe

Food is a symbol of showing love and that’s what we do at JamanvaR catering! Be it your birthday party, corporate meetings, get-togethers, kitty parties, launch parties, exhibitions, baby showers, karwa chauth, farewell, or as big as the best live event of your life – your wedding day, we’ve got your back! Whether you are planning an elaborate, traditional Indian wedding or one celebrating multiple faiths, the food that you choose to serve will be remembered long after your big day.
Our journey in creating your perfect event begins by listening to you and your family, understanding the unique elements of your culture, and the role you want food to play in your celebration. From this knowledge, our team of young professionals—qualified and experienced chefs and food specialists—create unique, inspired menus leaving no detail overlooked.

We treat our clients like our own family. This shows up in the service that we provide. Our motto is to enable our clients to relax and enjoy a hassle-free meal. Talking of the food we have tremendously talented and skillful chefs genuinely competent to prepare all kinds of cuisines. Whether it is North Indian, South Indian, Gujarati, Chinese, Italian cuisine, or a mix of many that you desire, our chefs leave no stone unturned to obtain the freshest produce, choose the finest ingredients and handpick the most fragrant spices. This quest for perfection inspires us to create authentic-tasting dishes prepared innovatively, spurs us to re-imagine traditional cuisine, and to combine subtle simplicity with contemporary flair.

Why Choose JamanvaR?

We are widely recognized for our standards of service, creativity, and exemplary cuisine. Constantly innovating, creating, and redefining the way of serving delectable food and efficient catering services, JamanvaR Catering is setting industry benchmarks for others to follow. Whether it is a small private gathering, a cocktail party, a big reception, or a destination wedding, our commitment and attention to detail remain the same at every stage - from the initial inquiry to flawless execution on the day of the event.

  • Hygiene and cleanliness is our priority.
  • Wide range of flexible food options.
  • Availability of full-fledged indoor and outdoor services.
  • Ability of our chefs to bring soul to the recipe.
  • Experienced and groomed staff.
  • Updated with new market trends.
  • Variety of themes as per the occasion.
  • Undivided attention and time to our clients.

We stand by ‘health is wealth’ by using only farm-fresh vegetables and fruits. We have professional teams for all kinds of food services - local, national, or international. You name it, we have it!
